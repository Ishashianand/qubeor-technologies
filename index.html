Hardware Meets Deep Neural Networks

Most technology firms write software. Most manufacturing plants build metal. At Qubeor Technologies, we engineer both.

Operating under the official classification of Manufacture of other special-purpose machinery, we design custom hardware architectures—from embedded biometric multi-sensors to autonomous environmental monitors—and integrate them seamlessly with highly optimized, real-time artificial intelligence.

We do not rely on off-the-shelf enclosures. Every component, PCB routing, and thermal dissipation system is built with mission-critical durability in mind.

Built on a Foundation of Advanced Engineering

🧠

Edge AI & Deep Learning

Deploying highly optimized, quantised (INT8/FP16) TensorFlow and PyTorch models directly onto edge devices (TFLite, ONNX Runtime). We enable zero-latency threat classification, fatigue detection, and real-time inference without relying on constant cloud connectivity.

⚙️

Advanced IoT & Sensor Fusion

Complex multi-sensor arrays (10+ sensors integrating I2C, SPI, UART, and GPIO) engineered to process biometric, environmental, and spatial data concurrently using Python asyncio for maximum throughput and fault tolerance.

📡

Secure Distributed Networks

Architecting horizontally scalable telemetry systems using AWS and microservices. We specialize in AES-256 encrypted LoRa mesh networks for off-grid operations and high-availability RESTful APIs that process high-frequency sensor streams with 99.5% uptime.
